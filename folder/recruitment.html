Join the Student Media Club!

Are you interested in film, sports broadcasting, podcasting, or livestreaming? The Student Media Club is the perfect place for you to explore your passion for media production and gain hands-on experience.

Join us to be part of a creative and collaborative community where you can learn new skills, make friends, and create amazing content together!
