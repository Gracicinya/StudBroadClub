Our Work

We are proud of the work we do at the Student Media Club. Here are some highlights of our projects and events.

Campus Film Festival

Every year, we host a campus film festival where students can showcase their short films and documentaries. It's a great opportunity for students to share their creativity and storytelling skills with the campus community.

Live Sports Broadcasts

We provide live broadcasts of our school's sports events, giving students the chance to experience the excitement of live production. From camera operation to live directing, there's a role for everyone in our sports broadcasting team.

Podcast Series

Our podcast team produces a variety of shows covering topics from campus news to pop culture. It's a great way for students to develop their audio production skills and share their voices with the community.

Live Streaming Events

Our live streaming team broadcasts events and activities happening on campus in real-time. It's a great way for students to learn about live production and engage with the broader community.

/>

These are just a few examples of the amazing work our members have done. We are always looking for new projects and ideas, so if you have a passion for media production, we encourage you to join us and be part of our creative community!
