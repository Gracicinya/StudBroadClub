What We Do

There are many ways to get involved in the Student Media Club. Whether you're interested in film, sports, audio, or livestreaming, there's something for everyone!

  • Film & Video

    We create short films, documentaries, and more. Learn how to use professional equipment and software. You can learn cinematography, and storytelling, how to edit, and more!

  • Sports Broadcasting

    We broadcast live sports events on campus. Cover games, tournaments, and other athletic events.

  • Podcasting and Audio

    We produce podcasts and audio content. Learn storytelling through sound and voice.

  • Livestreaming

    We livestream events and activities on campus. Learn how to set up and manage live broadcasts and engage live audiences.

Find Your Role

Are you interested in film, sports, audio, livestreaming or just want to flex? There's something for you - no experience needed!

  • Director/Producer

    Oversee the creative vision and production process.

    Plan and coordinate projects, manage teams, and ensure the final product meets the club's standards.

  • Cinematographer/Camera Operator

    Handle the camera work and visual storytelling.

    Capture the action, frame the shots, and work closely with the director to bring the vision to life.

  • Editor

    Assemble and edit footage to create a cohesive final product.

    Use editing software to cut, arrange, and enhance the footage, adding effects, music, and sound to create a polished final product.

  • Audio Engineer

    Manage the audio aspects of productions.

    Record, mix, and enhance audio to ensure clear and high-quality sound in broadcasts and productions.

  • Livestream Technician

    Set up and manage live broadcasts.

    Handle the technical aspects of livestreaming, including setting up equipment, managing the broadcast, and troubleshooting any issues that arise during live events.

  • Graphic Designer

    Create visual content for broadcasts, events, and social media.

    Design promotional materials, graphics, and branding elements to support the club's activities.

  • Social Media Manager

    Manage the club's social media presence.

    Create and schedule posts, engage with followers, and promote the club's activities and events on various social media platforms.

  • Scriptwriter

    Write scripts for films, documentaries, and other productions.

    Develop compelling narratives and dialogue that align with the club's vision and goals.

  • On Air Talent

    Participate in live broadcasts and events.

    Act as a host, anchor, or guest on live shows and events.
